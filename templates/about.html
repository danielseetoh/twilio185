About Us

Our Vision

Empowering community, saving lives.


Our Mission

To expedite victim's treatment during emergency and work towards a more health-conscious society.


Our Partners

Twilio is a cloud communication company. It allows users to use its application program interface (API) to communicate with others through text messages, phone calls and even video calls. In LifeLine, Twilio enables the exchange of text messages between bystanders or victims with the nearby responders.


Our History

Development of LifeLine started in 2016 during our Social Entrepreneurship class. We were tasked to improve the communication system during an emergency with the help of Twilio as the cloud communication platform. After doing extensive market research and user validation, we unanimously agreed to target high-density cities because ambulances are more likely to be caught in traffic congestion and there is a higher chance of geting a nearby responder in the event of an emergency. Singapore has been chosen as our first target market.


Our Background

We are a group of engineers of diverse skills from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. In another three semesters, we are going to graduate with a Bachelor of Engineering and a Master of Science in Technology Management.
