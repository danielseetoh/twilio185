Services

LifeLine is a multi-functional app which primarily aims to get nearby responders to help victims while waiting for the ambulance to arrive. We believe that every single minute can make a difference in a victim's life, and this is the very foundation of our solution. We also offer a handy first aid kit manual to assist users when diagnosing symptoms or performing basic treatments for mere injuries as well as emergency cases. Ultimately, LifeLine strives towards becoming the first solution that people will think of when they are facing an emergency.
